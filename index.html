Trusting Us With Your Reputation

Signature Long Haul is the Phoenix that has risen from the ashes of the former Scheduled Long Haul team at Thomas Cook. Scheduled Long Haul employed a team of 70 people and was one the divisions of the Thomas Cook group that remained extremely successful until the collapse of the business. We are pleased to have taken on a large proportion of the old team and will be offering the same amazing product and service that agents who dealt with Thomas Cook Long Haul have become accustomed to. The Scheduled Long Haul Division was previously known as Thomas Cook Signature and our new brand pays homage to the old and new name.

Over 300 years of combined experience

Our incredible team have years of experience of gained at the Thomas Cook Scheduled Long Haul Team. Some of the team have been booking long haul holidays for over 40 years and enjoy nothing more than designing a complex itinerary.

We never say No

We invite agents to come to us with their most complex enquiries. Our team is not managed based on the number of bookings they do- they are managed based on the quality of the service they give.

Hand Picked Resorts

Everything we feature has been handpicked . Our team will have an in-depth knowledge of every property featured and will be able to advise suitability of each based on your client needs.

Beat the Direct Sell

By focusing on specific product, we can contract directly with the product supplier. Most of our competitors now buy accommodation through a bed bank who keep their own margin adding a layer of additional cost on the price your client pays.

Trade Only

We don’t deal with the public. We know that as agents you have to provide the customer with the principal ATOL holders invoice. It is a fact that even the most honest tour operators cannot stop a client coming to book with them directly in the future once the customer knows who the principal is..

Rewards

The Miles High Club is our reward programme which gives you up to 1.5 miles for every £1 of revenue you do with us. £240,000 revenue a year with us would be enough for you to enjoy a free holiday worth up to £3,300 without paying a penny.
